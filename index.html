Frequently Asked Questions

01

What size dumpster do I need?

The right size depends on your project and the amount of waste. Our team can help you choose the best dumpster for your needs.

02

How long can I keep the dumpster?

You can keep the dumpster for the duration of your project. We offer flexible rental periods and can arrange extensions if needed.

03

What materials can go in the dumpster?

Most household, construction, and renovation waste can go in the dumpster. Certain hazardous materials may not be allowed, so contact our team.

04

Do you deliver outside Charlottetown?

Yes, we provide dumpster delivery services in Charlottetown and surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm availability in your location.
